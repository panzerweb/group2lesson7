Sector-Specific Insights

Agriculture Report

Insights on agricultural-specific

  • Limited land ownership opportunities
  • Fewer inheritance rights than male farmers
  • Land ownership is crucial for income and household autonomy

Industry and Manufacturing

Insights on industrial-specific

  • 64% of economic zone workers are women
  • High representation in electronics and apparel industries
  • Concerns about employment conditions in export processing zones

Tourism

Insights on tourism-specific

  • Limited access to front-of-house positions
  • Women are often relegated to housekeeping and service roles
  • Educational and language skill barriers

Government Services

Insights on government-specific

  • Better pay and conditions than many industrial sectors
  • Women concentrated in stereotypical care sectors
  • Underrepresentation in higher-paying positions

Business Outsourcing

Insights on Outsourcing-specific

  • Technology roadmap 2011-2016 lacks gender-responsive policies
  • Limited access to higher-paid technical roles
  • Barriers to advancement in IT and engineering

Entrepreneurship

Insights on entrepreneur-specific

  • Rising female entrepreneurs in developing nations.
  • Women-led MSMEs boost jobs and development.
  • More women entrepreneurs than men, driven by necessity.
